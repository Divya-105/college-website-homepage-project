Our Specialities

MIT Academy of Engineering (MITAOE) is one of the prestigious Autonomous Institute affiliated to Savitribai Phule Pune University. At MITAOE, curriculum development, content delivery and learner assessment are the key fundamentals for the success of academics and the teaching-learning process.

THE TEACHING-LEARNING PROCESS REVOLVES AROUND THE FOLLOWING POINTS:

Achieving comprehensive upgrades with multiple improvements by introducing Project-based Learning. Diminishing four-wall classroom concept by planning, critical thinking, reasoning, and creativity. Providing real-time, customizable and flexible criteria-based evaluation system illuminating students’ performance. Enabling students to connect with domain experts by tying up with distinguished companies. Focusing on use of ICT for learning and sustainable improvements.

CONTENT DELIVERY FOCUSSES ON THE FOLLOWING POINTS:

Providing excellent teaching at all levels in a research-oriented environment to develop the students intellectually and individually. Producing graduates who are adaptable and alert to the benefits of lifelong learning and those who meet the requirements of employers from all sectors. Maintaining a balance of basic and strategic research and promoting learning through the application of knowledge. Optimizing the use of resources to improve the working environment and range of services for students and staff.

Our facilities

MITAOE offers full-time, undergraduate programs (B. Tech) in Chemical, Civil, Computer, Electronics, ENTC, IT and Mechanical Engineering.

World Class Library

The Central Library at MITAOE preserves and provides access to a rich, diverse and enduring source of knowledge to inform, inspire and engage users in user intellectual and creative initiatives. It provides access to information, services and opportunities that enable, enrich and boost the quality of life for all its users.

AjaanVriksha

AjaanVriksha is the official Annual Cultural Magazine of MIT Academy of Engineering. Founded in 2014 it provides a creative platform for the students to present themselves as well as voice their opinions. While publishing articles in Hindi, English, and Marathi it also has a special section for Arts and Photography as well.

Corporate Relations

The Corporate Relations Office (CRO) at MITAOE aims at providing its students a comprehensive platform for different curricular and cocurricular activities such as Student Internship Program (SIP), Semester Long Internship Program (SLIP), Industry Sponsored Projects, Student Development Programs like Seminars, Workshops, Guest Lecture Series, Industrial Visits, Add-On Courses etc. in collaboration with various renowned Industries and Institutions. To make MITAOE students Industry Ready, Industry Experts from prominent industries and Professors from Well-known Institutions are involved in Curriculum Design as well as for Course Delivery in each academic school.
